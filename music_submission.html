Music Submission

中文說明

Description of payment for submision

    Fee free for music submission.
    People who will not attend the conference but music pieces are selected should pay the “Conference Registration fee”
    Registration fee (either the “Works Selected Registration”, or the whole 2017 WOCMAT Conference Registration) is required for the composers to perform the pieces in the concert.

CategoryＡ: Call for works for Concert

    1、Tape Music, Fixed Media, Acousmatic Music 
    2、Mixed Work for solo 
    3、Live Electronics, without instruments(with acoustical instrument) 
    4、Multimedia Work 
    5、Soundscape Composition 

    Note: 
    --Works must be no longer than 8 minutes. 
    --Instrument list**：Flute, Violin, Cello, Piano, Erhu, Zhongruan, and Zheng. 
    **If there is an instrument not provided above, the composer should provide the player himself. 

Category B : Call for International Electroacoustic Music Young Composer Award

    WOCMAT call for 2019 International Electroacoustic Music Young Composer Award Competition, the 2 first prize works will be performed in conference concert.Award-winners will be offered a WOCMAT official certificate. 


    1、Electronic Art Music: Synthesized music, Computer Algorithmic music, Soundscape composition, Tape music, Fixed media, Acousmatic music 
    2、Applied Music: Commercial music, MIDI music, Digital music, Film music 

    Note: 
    --Length of Work must be between 90 seconds and 5 minutes. 
    --composers under the age of 35 - born after 1984/10/1 

General rules:

    -- Submission Deadline: September 30, 2019, 6 pm (UTC+9) 
    --Up to two works may be submitted, but they must belong to different sub-category 
    -- Only Online Submissions are allowed. If your file over 100MB , please give the link of your work through the form from the submission system. 
    --Media Files - Audio file must be in stereo (either mp3, AIFF, or WAV), the video file in format : mp4, mov, or avi 
    --category A, item 2 and 3: must online upload the audio file of complete work, and/or related files(patches, documents, programs, etc.) 
    --category A, item 2: must online upload the score (PDF) 

音樂作品投稿

english version

投稿費用說明

    -- 音樂作品投稿時暫無需繳費。 
    -- 只參加音樂作品競賽而不參加研討會/工作坊者，伺入選後需要繳交 "研討會註冊費"  
    -- 只參加音樂作品競賽而不參加研討會/工作坊者，伺入選後需要繳交 "研討會註冊費"  
    -- 只有繳交研討會註冊費或工作坊註冊費者，大會才會將作品納入演出。

CategoryＡ 音樂會作品徵選

    1、純電子音樂 
    2、器樂與電子音樂 (限一種獨奏樂器，參下方樂器別) 
    3、現場電子音樂表演(不用任何傳統樂器) 
    4、多媒體音樂 
    5、音景作品 

    Note: 
    --作品長度限制8分鐘以內
    --器樂演出種類限制：Flute, Violin, Cello, Piano, Erhu, Zhongruan, and Zheng. 
    **非以上樂器請表演者自備 

CategoryＢ「國際青年電子音樂創作奬」徵選

    國際青年電子音樂創作奬徵選，各類首獎作品將獲得演出於大會音樂會之機會，獲獎作曲家將獲頒大會獲獎證書。

    1、 藝術類類：電子音樂/電腦合成音樂/電腦演算音樂/音景作品 
    2、流行應用類：商用配樂, MIDI音樂, 數位音樂, 電影配樂 

    Note: 
    --作品長度限制為5分鐘之內 
    --投稿作曲家限年齡於西元1984年10月1日後出生三十五歲以下者。

投稿規則：

    -- 截稿日期：9月30日, 2019, 6 pm (UTC+9) 
    -- 作品格式：音檔必須為Stereo立體聲（WAVE,或AIFF,或mp3, or MP4/avi/mov）
    -- 至多可繳交兩件作品，但需屬於不同子目錄 
    -- 限線上online 投稿，報名表與檔案連結，寄至：XXX@gmail.com 
    -- 線上投稿之檔案連結，建議透過dropbox.com, wetransfer.com
    -- 目錄A之子項目2 與3: 全曲錄音檔與(或)相關檔案(程式，文件…等) 
    -- 目錄A之子項目2: 樂譜 (PDF檔)
